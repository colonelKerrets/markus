    What drives me?

    I design the experience between users and technology. A beautiful design for me is not only a beautiful visual design, but also adding value to the users by simplifying processes, reducing noise and solving their problems.


    How do I reach these goals?

    I base design decisions on user insights, consider opinions of cross functional team members and reach my design goals by iteration.


    How do I keep up with the fast paced digital world?

    I believe in the collaborative mind of a team. Challenge and to be challenged for the sake of improvement is my daily routine. My high level of interpersonal skills and experience provides a catalyst for efficient team work.
